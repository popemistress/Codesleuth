# Traceability is Trust: Digital Maturity in Food & Beverage

In the food and beverage industry, trust is the currency. A single contamination event or unlabeled allergen can destroy a brand's reputation overnight and lead to severe regulatory penalties. As consumers demand transparency from farm to fork, legacy paper-based tracking systems are becoming an existential liability.

## The Cost of opacity
When a recall happens, speed is critical. Companies relying on disjointed ERPs and spreadsheets struggle to identify the specific batch, supplier, or production line responsible. This results in blanket recalls that waste perfectly safe product and massive financial losses.
