# The Hardware is Hard, but the Software is Critical

In consumer electronics, the differentiator is no longer just the device—it's the ecosystem. Smart speakers, wearables, and connected appliances live or die by the quality of their software. Yet, firmware development often lags behind modern web practices, suffering from manual testing and infrequent release cycles.

## The IoT Security Gap
Connected devices are a primary target for cyberattacks. Shipping a device with hardcoded credentials or unpatched vulnerabilities is a disaster waiting to happen. Security cannot be an afterthought; it must be baked into the CI/CD pipeline of the firmware itself.
