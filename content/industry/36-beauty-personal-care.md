# Beauty Tech: Beyond the Surface

The beauty industry is tech-driven. From AR virtual try-ons to AI-powered skin analysis and personalized formulation, technology is central to the customer experience. However, beneath the glossy frontend lies complex requirements for formulation safety, ingredient compliance, and subscription logistics.

## The Compliance Challenge
Different regions have vastly different regulations for cosmetic ingredients. A formula compliant in the US might be banned in the EU. Managing these rules across global SKUs requires intelligent software that validates formulations against regional databases in real-time.
