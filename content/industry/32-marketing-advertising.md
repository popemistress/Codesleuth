# Why Marketing Agencies Cannot Scale With Spreadsheet Chaos

Modern marketing is no longer about just great creative work; it's about data, speed, and precision execution at scale. Yet, most agencies and in-house marketing teams are still managing multimillion-dollar budgets using disconnected spreadsheets, email threads, and fragmented tools. This operational chaos creates a ceiling on growth and introduces critical risks in compliance and brand safety.

## The Operational Bottleneck
As campaigns span across social, search, display, and influencer channels, the complexity of approval workflows explodes. A single creative asset might need sign-off from legal, brand, and the client before going live. Without a centralized system, version control fails, incorrect assets get published, and budget pacing goes unnoticed until it's too late.
