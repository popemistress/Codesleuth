# Digital Transformation on the Runway

The fashion industry is undergoing a massive shift from fast fashion to sustainable, digital-first models. Brands are embracing 3D design, digital sampling, and transparent supply chains to meet the demands of modern consumers. However, legacy PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) systems are often clunky and disconnected from the e-commerce frontend.

## The Sustainability Information Gap
Consumers want to know where their clothes come from. Proving sustainability claims requires tracing raw materials from the cotton field to the retail shelf. This data challenge requires sophisticated software architecture that crosses organizational boundaries.
